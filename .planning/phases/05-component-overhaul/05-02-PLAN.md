With: bg-[var(--color-accent-muted)]

Replace: bg-gradient-to-r from-primary-blue to-accent-purple (solid fills on badges/avatars)
With: bg-[var(--color-accent)]

<!-- Spacing grid (8px multiples) -->
Valid Tailwind spacing: p-1(4px) p-2(8px) p-3(12px) p-4(16px) p-6(24px) p-8(32px) p-10(40px) p-12(48px) p-16(64px) p-20(80px) p-24(96px)
Valid gaps: gap-1 gap-2 gap-3 gap-4 gap-6 gap-8 gap-10 gap-12 gap-16
Section padding: py-24 (96px) is the standard, py-16 (64px) for smaller sections
Card padding: p-6 (24px) or p-8 (32px)
Note: p-3(12px) and p-1(4px) are acceptable at small scale but prefer p-2/p-4/p-6/p-8 when possible

- Experience/duration labels (e.g., "3+ years", "Expert")
- Architecture description labels
- Year badges in timeline
- Process step numbers
